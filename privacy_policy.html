Terms and Conditions

Introduction

Welcome to Ride-Share. we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our carpooling services.

Information We Collect

1.User Registration Information: When you sign up for an account with [Your Carpooling Website Name], we collect personal information, including your name, email address, phone number, and other relevant details.

2.Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information for your user profile, such as a profile picture, car details, and ride preferences. This information is displayed to other users of our platform.

3.Ride Information: When you use our carpooling services, we collect data related to your rides, including pickup and drop-off locations, ride dates and times, route information, and payment details.

4.Communication Data: We may collect and store your communications with other users, as well as any feedback, reviews, or comments you provide. 5.Device and Usage Information: We automatically collect information about the devices you use to access our services, including IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and referring URLs. We also collect data on how you interact with our website and mobile apps, such as pages visited and features used.

Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience, analyze usage patterns, and serve relevant advertisements. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including but not limited to:

1.Providing Carpooling Services: To facilitate ridesharing, including matching drivers with passengers, calculating fares, and ensuring a smooth carpooling experience.

2.Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, address concerns, and provide customer support.

3.Safety and Security: To verify user identities, monitor for fraudulent activity, and ensure the safety of our users.

4.Improving Services: To analyze user behavior, conduct research, and enhance our website and mobile apps.

5.Marketing and Communications: To send you updates, promotions, and newsletters, where you have opted in to receive such communications. You can opt out at any time.

6.Legal Compliance: To comply with legal requirements and respond to lawful requests from government authorities.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information with the following third parties:

1.Other Users: Your profile information, including your name and profile picture, is shared with other users when you participate in carpooling.

2.Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to assist us in delivering our services, such as payment processors, customer support, and marketing partners.

3.Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or respond to legal requests from authorities.

Business Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to a new entity.

Your Privacy Choices

You have several privacy choices, including:

1.Account Settings: You can review and update your account settings and profile information at any time.

2.Marketing Communications: You can opt out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails we send you.

3.Cookies: You can control cookie settings in your browser to manage how cookies are used.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on our website, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated.
